Statistics

Core Statistics Sources

  • EIU Data Services
    Choose CountryData, Market Indicators and Forecasts, or CityData from the EIU menu.
  • OECD Statistics
    OECD Stat enables users to search for and extract data from OECD databases. Also known for its International Energy Agency Statistics.
  • International Monetary Fund Data
    Int'l Financial Statistics, Balance of Payments, Direction of Trade, Govt Finance Statistics, and more.
    TIP: Use the "Query Builder"
  • UNdata
    A variety of statistical resources of the United Nations..
  • UNCTAD Stat
    Stats from the United Nations Conference on Trade and Development
  • World dataBank (World Bank)
    Includes World Devt. Indicators, Global Development Finance, African Devt. Indicators and more.
  • Global Financial Data
    Covering 300 years, GFD tracks the growth of the European, US and global economies in the 1800s and 1900s for both developing and emerging markets.To use GFD, you must create an account, which is free. You will be assigned a unique User Name and Password using your e-mail.
  • Passport GMID Euromonitor
    To access data, click on "Search", then "Menu Search". First select your category and country. After retrieving your search, you can adjust the time series, getting data back to 1977.
  • Global Insight
    Click on "DataInsight-Web". Includes data from IMF, central banks, and more.

Country Research

Country Research

  • Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU)
    Select Navigation by Country from the menu. EIU's country reports are especially rich sources of background information and current analysis.
  • Europa World
  • IMF eLibrary
    Search for publications and data from the International Monetary Fund.
  • Statesman's Yearbook
    Online from vol.1, 1864 - present. Some print editions: JA51 .S7
  • Political Handbook of the World
    Comprehensive country profiles chronicling national history, government, and political parties.
  • Roubini Global Economics (RGE Monitor)
    Click on the Economies dropdown menu for region and country-specific options
  • Country Studies from the Library of Congress
    Though this series is updated only sporadically, the information is useful for historical context.
  • CIA World Factbook

News Sources

News Sources

  • Current News
  • Historical News
  • LexisNexis Academic
    Full text news source.
  • ProQuest Newsstand
    Full text of news sources, including The New York Times, Wall Street Journal, and Washington Post.
  • Access World News
    Aggregated headlines and a search engine of many of the world's news sources.
  • Press Display
    Full image, includes tabloids. Latest 60 days only.
  • Google News
    Aggregated headlines and a search engine of many of the world's news sources.
  • Proquest Historical Newspapers
    Search New York Times, Washington Post, Christian Science Monitor, Wall St. Journal, The Guardian UK, and more, back to the 19th century.
  • America's Historical Newspapers (1690 -1922)
    Covering 1690 - 1876, this database present full PDF images of the papers. JHU has access to Series I only.
  • Historical Newspapers Online
    Indexes the Times of London, NY Times, and more.
  • Foreign Broadcast Information Service (FBIS) Daily Reports, 1950-1996
    English translations of radio and tv broadcasts and news articles worldwide. A valuable source for primary research. Articles from 1974-1996 are included.
  • World News Connection: 1995-2013
    Continuation of FBIS Daily Reports. Ceased publication Dec 31, 2013.
  • Financial Times Historical Archive, 1888-2010
    One of the world's most authoritative daily business newspapers.

Government Publications

Government Publications (U.S.)

  • ProQuest Congressional
    Access to fulltext Congressional hearings, CRS reports, Congressional Record and other congressional documents, back to 1789.
  • FDsys
    Official publications from all three branches of the US Federal Government.
  • Catalog of US Government Publications
    Index of US government publications 1976 to present. Preceded by Monthly Catalog of US Govt Publications.
  • NTIS (National Technical Information Service)
    Source for accessing unclassified reports from US (and some int'l) government agencies. If fulltext is not available, try Google, see a librarian, or request on interlibrary loan.

International Organizations

  • Access UN
    Indexes UN documents back to 1945, with some fulltext. For more fulltext, use the UN Official Documents System. Most documents not in fulltext are on microfiche held at the JHU's MSE Library and can be requested.
  • UN Official Documents System
    Fulltext of all United Nations parliamentary documents back to 1993 and resolutions back to 1946.
  • OECD iLibrary
    Fulltext online library of the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development.
  • World Bank eLibrary
    Fulltext access to thousands of World Bank publications.
